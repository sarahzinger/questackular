    Help!
  • Create a quest title!
  • Give your quest a cool description!
  • Pick whether you you want the quest to start as active or inactive!
    • Active quests can be played immediately, but cannot be edited (until made inactive).
    • Inactive quests can not be played immediately, but you still can edit them (to make them more awesome!).
  • Click 'New Step' to give your quest some steps!
    • You can't create steps without making a quest first.
  • Click 'Save' so others can play your awesome quest (don't forget to make it 'active')!
  • Or click 'Clear' to cancel.
